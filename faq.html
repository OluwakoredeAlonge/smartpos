General Questions

What is UbiquePOS?

+

UbiquePOS is a comprehensive cloud-based point-of-sale system designed to automate business operations, prevent theft, and provide real-time insights. Perfect for retail stores, pharmacies, restaurants, supermarkets, and hotels, it combines inventory management, sales tracking, and multi-branch support in one intuitive platform.

Who can benefit from UbiquePOS?

+

Any business that sells products or services can benefit, including retail stores, pharmacies, restaurants, supermarkets, hotels, boutiques, and service providers. Whether you're a single location or managing multiple branches, UbiquePOS scales to your needs.

What makes UbiquePOS different from other POS systems?

+

UbiquePOS enforces accountability by preventing sales without stock updates, offers remote management from anywhere, provides real-time low-stock alerts, and includes built-in e-commerce expansion capabilities. Plus, you get free hosting and domain with built-in SEO tools at no extra cost.

Features & Functionality

How does UbiquePOS prevent theft?

+

By enforcing strict accountability: no sales can be processed without stock updates. Every transaction requires proper inventory verification, role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can perform specific actions, comprehensive audit logs track all activities, and admins can approve sales remotely. This multi-layered approach eliminates unauthorized sales and theft.

Can I manage multiple branches?

+

Absolutely! UbiquePOS supports unlimited branches with centralized control. Monitor all locations from one dashboard, sync inventory in real-time across branches, view consolidated or branch-specific reports, and easily add new locations as you expand. Growth has never been easier.

What reports and analytics are available?

+

Comprehensive real-time reports including sales history by date/product/attendant, top-selling products analysis, low-stock alerts, revenue trends and performance metrics, inventory turnover rates, and exportable data for further analysis. All designed to drive data-driven decisions.

Does UbiquePOS work offline?

+

UbiquePOS requires an internet connection for real-time synchronization and remote management features. However, the system is designed to work efficiently even with moderate internet speeds (10-30 Mbps), and uses minimal data (under 3GB per month).

Setup & Implementation

What are the system requirements?

+

Hardware: Any PC or laptop with 4GB RAM (8GB recommended), or even an Android phone with a modern browser. Internet: Stable connection with 10-30 Mbps speed. Printer: Thermal receipt printer for professional receipts (optional but recommended).

How long does setup take?

+

Just 1-2 days for complete deployment: Hour 1: Sign up and configure business details. Hours 2-3: Bulk import inventory via Excel (no manual entry needed). Hour 4: Assign user roles and conduct training. Hour 5: Test runs and fine-tuning. You'll be fully operational in 48 hours or less!

Do I need technical skills to use UbiquePOS?

+

No technical skills required! UbiquePOS is designed with an intuitive interface that anyone can use. We provide guided training during setup, and our support team is always available to help. If you can use a web browser, you can use UbiquePOS.

Can you help migrate my existing data?

+

Yes! We offer free data migration assistance. Simply provide your existing inventory data in Excel or CSV format, and our team will help import it into UbiquePOS. We ensure a smooth transition with zero data loss.

Security & Data

How secure is my data?

+

Highly secure. We use encrypted cloud storage, SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission, role-based access controls, comprehensive audit logs, regular automated backups, and compliance with data protection standards. Your business data is protected with bank-level security.

What happens if I lose internet connection?

+

As a cloud-based system, UbiquePOS requires internet connectivity for full functionality. We recommend having a backup internet source (mobile hotspot) for uninterrupted service. The system uses minimal bandwidth, so even mobile data can keep you running.

Can I export my data?

+

Yes! You own your data and can export it anytime in Excel/CSV format. Export sales reports, inventory lists, customer data, and more for external analysis or record-keeping. Your data is always accessible and portable.
