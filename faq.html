What is UbiquePOS?

+

UbiquePOS is a comprehensive cloud-based point-of-sale system designed to automate business operations, prevent theft, and provide real-time insights. Perfect for retail retail, pharmacies, restaurants, supermarkets, and hotels, it combines inventory management, sales tracking, and multi-branch support in one intuitive platform.

Who can benefit from UbiquePOS?

Any business that sells products or services can benefit, including retail stores, pharmacies, restaurants, supermarkets, hotels, boutiques, and service providers. Whether you're a single location or managing multiple branches, UbiquePOS scales to your needs.

Features & Functionality

How does UbiquePOS prevent theft?

+

By enforcing strict accountability: no sales can be processed without stock updates. Every transaction requires proper inventory verification, role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can perform specific actions, and comprehensive audit logs track all activities. This multi-layered approach eliminates unauthorized sales and theft.

Can I manage multiple branches?

+

Absolutely! UbiquePOS supports unlimited branches with centralized control. Monitor all locations from one dashboard, sync inventory in real-time across branches, view consolidated or branch-specific reports, and easily add new locations as you expand. Growth has never been easier.

What reports and analytics are available?

+

Comprehensive real-time reports including sales history by date/product/attendant, top-selling products analysis, low-stock alerts, revenue trends and performance metrics, inventory turnover rates, and exportable data for further analysis. All designed to drive data-driven decisions.

Does UbiquePOS work offline?

+

As a cloud-based system, UbiquePOS requires internet connectivity for full functionality. We recommend having a backup internet source (mobile hotspot) for uninterrupted service. The system uses minimal bandwidth, so even mobile data can keep you running.

Setup & Implementation

What are the system requirements?

+

Hardware: Any PC or laptop with 4GB RAM (8GB recommended), or even an Android phone with a modern browser. Internet: Stable connection with 10-30 Mbps speed. Printer: Thermal receipt printer for professional receipts (optional but recommended).

How long does setup take?

+

Just 1-2 days for complete deployment: Hour 1: Sign up and configure business details. Hours 2-3: Bulk import inventory via Excel (no manual entry needed). Hour 4: Assign user roles and conduct training. Hour 5: Test runs and fine-tuning. You'll be fully operational in 48 hours or less!

Do I need technical skills to use UbiquePOS?

+

No tech skills required! UbiquePOS is designed with an intuitive interface that anyone can use. We provide guided training during setup, and our support team is always available to help. If you can use a web browser, you can use UbiquePOS.

Can you help migrate my existing data?

+

Yes! We offer free data migration assistance. Simply provide your existing inventory data in Excel or CSV format, and our team will help import it into UbiquePOS. We ensure a smooth transition with zero data loss.
